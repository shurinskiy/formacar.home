Formacar is the first automotive ecosystem. By bringing together a number of services and products, Formacar gives you a new and wonderful experience of interacting with the automotive world. The Formacar app is the basis of our ecosystem that we have been developing since 2017. Here you will find:

  • A 3D Car Configurator with no peers worldwide. A vast selection of car models and all kinds of tuning components lets you create your ultimate dream car.
  • An Ads section for buying and selling cars and car parts all around the world.
  • A built-in social network: meet new people, interact and share content with your friends.
  • News: always stay tuned in on the hottest news of the automobile world.
  • AR Mode. You can try on various new wheels in this mode and even project your virtual 3D car into the real world to get an idea what it will look like once finished.
